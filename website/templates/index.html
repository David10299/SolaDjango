Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our sugaring services and what makes them the best choice for smooth, radiant skin. We’re here to answer your questions!

1. What is sugaring?

It’s a form of hair removal dating back to ancient Egypt. The Egyptians associated a hairless body as the epitome of beauty. A paste made of sugar, lemon, and water is used to remove the hair.

2. I’ve been waxed before. Is it the same?

No! Sugaring is way more gentle on the skin than wax. The sugar paste adapts to your body temperature, so there’s no risk of burning the skin. It only adheres to dead skin cells and hair, so there’s a lessened risk of lifting skin. Sugar can also adhere onto shorter hairs.

4. Is it painful?

Your first session won’t feel great, but it’s tolerable! I carve out plenty of time so you’re able to take small breaks, if needed.

6. I got my period, can I still get sugared?

Yes! To best prepare: Take a warm shower, take an ibuprofen 20-30 minutes before your session, and insert a new tampon or cup. I have tampons at the studio if you’re in need of one.

7. I’m pregnant, can I still get sugared?

Yes, and congratulations! Sugaring during pregnancy is a great option, especially before your belly begins to grow. Please note you may be more sensitized due to the extra blood flow in your body.
